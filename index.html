Development Economist

Joël
Cariolle

Ph.D  ·  HDR

Research Officer at FERDI and Affiliate Researcher at CERDI — University Clermont-Auvergne. My work investigates the digitalization process and its impact on economic development. I also work on economic vulnerability in Least Developed Countries, regional integration in sub-Saharan Africa, and corruption in developing economies.
